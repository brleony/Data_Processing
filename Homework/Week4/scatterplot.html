Tourism and Income Equality in Europe in 2015

Data Processing Week 4 Assignment

Source: Eurostat

The income equality of ever is displayed on the y axis. It is measured by the gini coefficient, a measure that can range from 0 to 100. A value above 50 is considered high, while 30 or above is considered medium and lower than 30 is considered low.
The value on the x axis, tourism, represents the percentage of the total population of the country that went on a trip abroad of 4 days or longer.
The region classification is according to the United Nations.
